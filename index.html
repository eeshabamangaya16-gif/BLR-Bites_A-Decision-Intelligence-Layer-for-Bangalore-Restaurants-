The model

Popularity, quality, reliability.

Popularity Visibility and public attention

Review volume is log-normalized so mainstream visibility is measured without letting giant counts overwhelm the platform.

Quality Bayesian-adjusted before scoring

Raw ratings are softened toward the platform average until a restaurant earns enough evidence.

Reliability Independent confidence

A place can be overhyped and reliable, or a hidden gem with lower confidence. The UI keeps that nuance visible.
